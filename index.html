Welcome To My Page!

Maia Dutta, MS, RD, LDN

I'm a clinical registered dietitian living in Nashville, TN with my husband and rescue kitties. I moved to Nashville from Oregon 10 years ago, but I've also lived in California, New York, and New Jersey.

I have a BS in biology with a minor in marine biology, worked in management at Starbucks for many years, and then decided to become a registered dietitian. I completed an MS in human nutrition last year.

Currently, I work from home as a private practice/consultant dietitian. In my "free time" I recently started learning web development and I love it!!

Please explore my website to learn more about my interests and background...and I'd love to hear from you, so send me an email or comment!
